The Challenge

Speed

Traditional sports predictors rely on manual content creation. During live matches, editors struggled to frame questions quickly enough for rapidly changing situations.

Relevance

Avoid repetitive or irrelevant questions. "Who will win the toss?" is already answered. The system needed to understand context and avoid stale predictions.

Engagement

Maintain excitement with context-aware predictions linked to real match moments. Static pre-match quizzes couldn't compete with the dynamic nature of live sports.

The AI Solution

LLM + Live Data Fusion

Autonomous Prediction Engine

Vinfotech designed an autonomous LLM engine integrated directly with live match data feeds. The system continuously reads match context—overs, player form, partnerships, pitch conditions, and momentum—and produces prediction questions in real time.

Key Features:

  • Context validation (no irrelevant questions)
  • Difficulty & excitement rating
  • Automatic market tagging
  • Multi-language generation
  • Continuous engagement metrics
